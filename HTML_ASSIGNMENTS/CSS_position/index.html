Static

No imapct of top,right,bottom and left

Relative

Moves from in normal postion to offset value

asas
position: relative;
top: 40px;
left: 40px;

Absolute(parent:relative)

Starts at position left:0; & top:0; of parent(not viewport) and then adds offset values

position: absolute;
top: 50px;
left: 80px;

fixed (parent:static)

Starts at positions left:0; & top:0; of viewport(containing block) and the adds offset values

position: fixed;
top:600px;
left: 175px;

sticky

position: sticky; top: 300px;

Initially position is relative, Once offset reachced on scroll position becomes fixed(at top:300px)
